Our Mission

At Pixie, we aim to make AI-powered photo editing accessible to everyone. By combining OpenCV with modern web technologies, we deliver tools that help users transform their images with ease and creativity.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, VisionEdit started as a small project to explore computer vision. Today, it has grown into a platform that empowers creators, students, and professionals to edit and enhance images instantly.
